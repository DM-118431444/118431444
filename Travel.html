Czech Republic


I visited Prague in Czech Republic about two years ago. Prague is probably the city I like the most out of the ones i have visited. Prague has very interesting history background. Czech Republic was a country that was constantly raided during war times and has since had its name changed many times. I learned a lot about the history of Prague when I visited because of the several walking tours that I went on. During our stay in Prague temperatures hit a record high of 42 degrees


Scotland


I visited Scotland about four years ago. I went with my family and we visited the city of Edinburgh. We visited during the Edinburgh fringe festival. This is a festival where over the space of a week there are many famous bands and comedians that perform in the city. We went to watch Neil Delamare perform.


England


I went the London to watch Chelsea play Manchester United in Stamford Bridge about five years ago. I got the tickets as a birthday present. There is plenty other things to do in London like to visit the tourist attractions such as Big Ben, The Tower of London and Buckingham Palace.
